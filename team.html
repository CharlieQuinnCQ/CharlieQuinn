Meet the team! 

Charlie Quinn consists of three people, Hidde Maalderink, Floor van Hagen and Lotte Adelaar, we are all creative business students at Breda University of Applied Sciences. We started this brand because we all agreed that there is too much inequality in the world. We also saw that certain people from certain gender get excluded by society, that is why we wanted to make a difference and start our brand, Charlie Quinn. 
Floor van Hagen

Floor van Hagen is our 24 year old head of management. She knows wear our hoodies our manufactured, our revenues streams, you name it she knows what’s going on! She also finds the best way for our brand to help and solve the problem of gender inequality.

Lotte Adelaar

Lotte Adelaar is our 19 year old head of marketing. She makes sure everyone knows about our brand! She doesn't only makes sure our hoodies are known but also that the message of our brand comes across to people all over the world via social media.  

Hidde Maalderink 

Hidde Maalderink is 22 years old and he is our head of production. He makes sure you can buy our hoodies whenever, wherever.  He came up with most of the lay-out of our website and spends hours trying to make our webstore even better than it already is!
